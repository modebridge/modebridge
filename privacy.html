ModeBRIDGE Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026  ·  Effective: June 5, 2026

1. Overview

The ModeBRIDGE app is a companion app for the ModeBRIDGE controller system. It is designed for use by adults (18 years of age or older) who own and operate the associated hardware. The app enables monitoring and control of vehicle systems including climate, power, tanks, and engine start via boost mode.

This app is not intended for use by minors. Do not allow children to use ModeBRIDGE. The app can trigger vehicle systems including engine start functions when paired with a remote start system.

2. Account & Data We Collect

ModeBRIDGE requires an account to use cloud-based features (remote MQTT access and push notifications). You may register with an email address and password, or sign in using Sign in with Apple.

Collected and stored on our servers:

  • Your email address (or an Apple-anonymized relay address if you use Sign in with Apple)
  • A hashed password (never stored in plain text; not applicable for Apple sign-in)
  • The unique identifier(s) of  hardware devices you have claimed
  • Your subscription status and billing period (managed via Stripe; see Section 5)
  • Your device's APNs push token (for offline push notification delivery)
  • Your notification preferences and alert thresholds
  • An app presence indicator (whether the app is currently active on cloud)

Stored only on your device:

  • Authentication tokens (access token in memory; refresh token in iOS Keychain, encrypted)
  • Wi-Fi network name and password for your ModeBRIDGE controller (stored in iOS Keychain, encrypted)
  • MQTT broker configuration (broker hostname and port)
  • Historical temperature and power readings from your ModeBRIDGE
  • Your in-app display preferences (temperature units, layout, color scheme)

Transmitted through our cloud broker during use:

  • ModeBRIDGE sensor readings (battery state of charge, tank levels, temperature, shore power status, rain sensor)
  • Control commands you issue (climate settings, light controls, vent position, etc.)

3. How Data Is Used

All data is used exclusively to operate your ModeBRIDGE account and ModeBRIDGE controller:

  • Your email and password authenticate you and secure access to your devices
  • Sensor readings are displayed in the app and used to trigger notifications you configure
  • Commands you send are relayed to your  hardware
  • Your APNs token enables push notifications when the app is closed
  • Notification preferences are used by the notification bridge to determine which alerts to send
  • Subscription status controls access to cloud-based features after your free trial period

Your data is never used for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond operating your account and device.

4. Data Storage & Retention

On your iPhone: Data is stored in iOS Keychain (credentials) and app storage (settings, history). It is removed when you delete the app or delete your account.

On our servers: Account information, device associations, and subscription status are stored on a private cloud server operated by the developer. Sensor data is relayed in real time and is not persistently stored on the broker beyond standard MQTT retained message behavior.

On the notification bridge: The bridge stores your APNs token and alert cooldown timestamps only. No sensor readings are retained. APNs tokens and notification preferences for devices that have not been active in 90 days are automatically purged from the bridge and broker. APNs tokens are also removed automatically when Apple indicates they are no longer valid.

6. Push Notifications

Push notifications are sent by a developer-operated notification bridge when your app is closed and a configured alert condition is met (e.g. low battery, rain detected). You can disable all push notifications or configure individual alert types within the app. Disabling notifications in the app prevents the bridge from sending pushes regardless of iOS notification permission settings.

7. Subscription & Billing

Remote cloud access (MQTT connection and push notifications) is available free during a 6-month trial period. After the trial, a subscription is required to continue using cloud features. Local control via Bluetooth or on the same Wi-Fi network is always available and never requires a subscription.

Billing is handled entirely by Stripe. We receive a subscription status from Stripe (active, trialing, expired) but never receive or store your payment card details. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time from the Settings screen in the app.

8. Security

  • Passwords are stored using a one-way hash — we cannot recover your password
  • Authentication tokens are short-lived (15-minute access tokens) and refreshed automatically
  • Device credentials and Wi-Fi passwords are stored in the iOS Keychain using system-level encryption
  • All cloud communication uses TLS 1.2 or higher encryption
  • Firmware downloads are validated using SHA-256 checksums before installation
  • No analytics SDKs, crash reporters, or advertising frameworks are included in the app

9. Age Requirement

ModeBRIDGE is intended for users 18 years of age or older. The app can control vehicle systems including engine start functions. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a minor has used this app to access vehicle systems, please contact us immediately at modebridge@gmail.com.

10. Account Deletion & Your Data Rights

You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Account → Delete Account within the app. Deleting your account:

  • Permanently removes your email, hashed password, and device associations from our servers
  • Cancels any active subscription (you will retain access until the end of the current billing period)
  • Publishes empty payloads to immediately clear all retained broker data for your devices
  • Removes your APNs token from the notification bridge

On-device data (history, settings, Keychain entries) is removed when you delete the app from your iPhone.

To request earlier removal of broker or bridge data without deleting your account, or if you cannot access the app, contact modebridge@gmail.com and we will process the request within 30 days.
